Annex VIII decision support

EU Device Classifier

Two related but separate EU regulations, two separate outputs. Pick the one that matches your product — general medical devices go through the MDR's 22 rules to land on Class I / IIa / IIb / III; in vitro diagnostics go through the IVDR's 7 rules to land on Class A / B / C / D.

Regulation (EU) 2017/745

EU MDR

General medical devices — instruments, implants, active equipment, software. Annex VIII, 22 classification rules across non‑invasive, invasive, active and special‑purpose devices.

Regulation (EU) 2017/746

EU IVDR

In vitro diagnostics — test kits, reagents, analysers, calibrators, companion diagnostics. Annex VIII, 7 classification rules driven by individual and public‑health risk.

Both frameworks work the same way underneath: you don't stop at the first rule that fits. You check every rule that could apply, and the highest resulting class wins. This tool asks one question at a time, quietly checks every relevant rule in the background, and shows you which one ends up governing the result — this is a decision‑support aid, not a substitute for a Notified Body or regulatory affairs professional.
